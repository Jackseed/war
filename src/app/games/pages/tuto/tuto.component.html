Welcome to War!

War is a simple strategic game.

This tutorial will explain you the rules.

Victory

To win, you have 2 possibilities:

1. Conquer the opponent castle

Victory

To win, you have 2 possibilities:

2. Kill all the opponent's units

Actions

You have 3 actions per turn.

Action is either a move or an attack.

Moves

Units can move once per turn.

Though Knights can move 2 tiles at once.

Range

Units can attack within their range.

Canons and archers attack 2 to 3 tiles away.

Sight

It's important to know the board.

Archers and knights have good vision.

Army

To conduct war, you select 12 battalions.
